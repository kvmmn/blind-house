Curated by Shahed Saffari
A Visual Excavation of Sadegh Hedayat

Sadegh Hedayat saw home not as a refuge, but as a haunting complex. This exhibition traces 150 years of isolation through the architecture of his paternal house, documented by two generations of Iranian masters.

The Publication.

We propose a definitive, bilingual monograph (Persian/English) that acts as the permanent archive of this project. Beyond the exhibition walls, this book will compile the unseen 1970s contact sheets of Bahman Jalali alongside the 2018 forensic photography of Mehdi Vosoughnia. It serves as a tangible preservation of a cultural site facing erasure.
